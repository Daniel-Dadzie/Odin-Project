Creamy Mango Smoothie

Description

Refreshing, tropical and healthy! This creamy mango smoothie is perfect for hot days or post-workout energy. Rich in vitamin and super easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 ripe mango (peeled and chopped)
  • q cup yogurt or milk
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Steps:

  1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
  2. Pour into a glass and serve cold.
