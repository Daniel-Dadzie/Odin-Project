Fluffy Pancakes

Description

A classic breakfast favorite! These pancakes are light, fluffy and easy to make. Perfect with syrup, butter or fresh fruits to start your morning with a smile.

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp melted butter
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk milk, egg and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients; mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a pan and pour ¼ cup batter per pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden.
