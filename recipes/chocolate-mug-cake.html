Chocolate Mug Cake

Description

Craving dessert in a hurry? This quick and gooey chocolate mug cake is ready in just 5 minutes. No oven needed-just a microwave and a sweet tooth!

Ingredients:

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • ¼ tsp baking powder
  • Chocolate chips (optional)

Step:

  1. Mix all ingredients in a mug.
  2. Microwave for 1 minute.
  3. Let cool slightly and enjoy!
